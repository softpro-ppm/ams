# To See the Errors in Console:

1. **Clear the filter:**
   - In the Console tab, find the filter box that says "Y console.log"
   - Click on it and DELETE the text (make it empty)
   - Or click the "X" icon to clear the filter

2. **Or click on the error indicators:**
   - On the right side of the console, you'll see:
     - Red circle with "2" (2 errors)
     - Orange triangle with "1" (1 warning)
   - **Click on the red "2 errors" circle** - this should show the errors

3. **Or change the filter levels:**
   - Click on "Default levels" dropdown
   - Make sure "Errors" and "Warnings" are checked
   - You might see "5 hidden" - click on that too

4. **Or type in console:**
   - Click in the console (where the > prompt is)
   - Type: `document.getElementById('root')`
   - Press Enter
   - What does it show?

Once you can see the errors, copy and paste them here!
